About Senyor

The most meaningful images come from real moments. My approach is simple: create a comfortable, relaxed space where you can be fully yourself—whether you're celebrating a milestone, embracing everyday life, or documenting love in all its forms.

I'm not here to force perfection; I'm here to capture connection, emotion, and the small in-between details that tell your story honestly and beautifully. Every session is guided by trust, intention, and a deep appreciation for the uniqueness of each person I photograph.

Photography is more than just pictures—it's preserving memories that deserve to be remembered and felt for years to come.
